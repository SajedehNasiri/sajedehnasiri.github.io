Indexturant

Project Overview

Engaged in a dynamic collaboration with Indextaurant, our team led the design of an e-commerce platform tailored to cater to the diverse needs of both individual coffee enthusiasts and industry professionals, including baristas and coffee shop owners.


This comprehensive online store offers all coffee-related essentials and accessories, providing a one-stop-shop experience for users across different categories.It also has various other services such as equipment rentals, maintenance of the equipment, and resale of refurbished industrial coffee shop equipment.


Our UX/UI design aims to build a user-friendly digital space, enhancing the experience for both coffee enthusiasts and industry professionals.

Discover

Understanding the core of the product:

Before doing any ideation we started to learn more about the business and their target by having regular meetings with business owner.

Business Needs:

Comprehensive Product Showcase: Present a diverse range of products, including coffee machines, coffee shop equipment, barista accessories, and representing Futura coffee machines in the UAE.

Service Portfolio Highlight: Showcase additional services like refurbishment of industrial coffee shop equipment, equipment rentals for events, and the sale of coffee beans and tea.

User Engagement and Contact Gathering: Encourage user interaction, exploration of services, and initially focus on collecting user contact details for future engagement.

Adaptability in Design: Design a platform capable of evolving from a contact-oriented site to a fully functional e-commerce platform.

Target Users:

Based on the marketing research conducted by the BO, we were provided with specific target user categories, outlined below:

  • Individual coffee lover (maybe Baristas)

  • Coffee shop owner (with his/her barista) who buys for his coffee shop (new or used equipment

  • Wholesale buyer (who buys used equipment)

  • Event managers/or similar who rents equipment for specific event/short period of time

Using the insights gained from the given data as well as secondary research, we created provisional personas to quickly identify our potential users. These provisional personas helped set the criteria for our interview participants and would be validated through user interviews.

Survey Insight

Now, it was time to validate these provisional personas through surveys and interviews with Indexturant customers. the survey was designed to fit the behavior of each user category aiming to identify:

  • 1. Thought processes and patterns

  • 2. Pain points

  • 3. General online shopping experiences

  • 4. Preferences

Survey takeaway

Key Survey Findings (Out of 140 respondents) What we did to fulfill this need
Purchase Preferences:
  • 80% showed a preference for in-person purchases
  • 74% expressed a desire to engage in discussions or consultations with professionals.
  • Locating "Contact Us" buttons strategically throughout the platform to build trust by assuring users of responsive assistance.
  • Clearly displayed all the information user need for easy access and prevent unnecessary calls.
Concerns About Used Equipment:
  • A significant number emphasized the importance of a guarantee, influencing decisions regarding used equipment purchases.
  • General service/checkup history was a crucial factor for those considering used equipment or coffee machines.
  • Visually highlighted the guarantee duration, maintenance, and related services strategically for clear visibility and user understanding.
Rental Considerations:
  • For rentals, factors such as brand, model, and price were identified as crucial decision-making elements.
  • Essential information were organized as FAQ section
  • A floating "Contact Us" button was provided
  • The "Request for Quote" section was provided as an option tailored to specific needs.
  • Showcased past event images for a visual overview

Usability:

Testing our design with potential users exposed a lack of intuitiveness, leading to unfinished tasks and user frustration. Addressing this, we emphasized to the client the risk of user dissatisfaction, highlighting the need to build trust. Our push for a clearer approach secured approval for an e-commerce model. However, the client insisted on collecting user contacts, creating a delicate balance between smooth online shopping and data collection.

Usability Test 1:

Moodboard:

Given that our primary focus revolves around coffee machines, be it sales of new or used machines for industrial or domestic purposes, we drew inspiration from the clean lines, minimalism, and elegance often associated with industrial design.

Our choice of red as one of the main colors reflects a gender-neutral palette, drawn directly from the vibrant hue of coffee cherries. Red not only symbolizes warmth but also exudes an energetic vibe, akin to the revitalizing effect of a freshly brewed cup of coffee.

We incorporated the soothing beige, a shade reminiscent of 'coffee with milk.' This subtle and comforting color complements the overall aesthetic, resonating with the familiarity and coziness of enjoying a cup of coffee.

In essence, our mood board encapsulates the essence of our business, blending industrial chic with the inviting warmth of coffee culture.
